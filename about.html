What SpellingBeeFinder Does

Every day, the NYT Spelling Bee publishes a new puzzle: 7 letters arranged in a honeycomb, one required center letter, and a word list nobody fully knows. SpellingBeeFinder solves any combination of 7 letters instantly and shows you every valid word you can make, with pangrams labeled and definitions available on click.

Beyond solving, the tool gives you progressive hints (how many words start with each letter, without spoiling the actual words) and a full practice mode for building vocabulary on any custom letter set.

How the Solver Works

Enter your 7 letters into the honeycomb grid. The center cell (highlighted in amber) is the required letter that must appear in every word. Click each outer cell and type its letter, then hit Find All Words.

The solver checks every combination against a curated English word list tuned to Spelling Bee rules: at least 4 letters, center letter included, only letters from the given set used. Results are ranked by length (longer words first) so the high-value pangrams are easy to spot.
