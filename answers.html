How Spelling Bee Scoring Works

Four-letter words are worth exactly 1 point each. Words with five or more letters score 1 point per letter. Pangrams — words that use all 7 letters at least once — earn a 7-point bonus on top of their letter count. Genius rank requires 70% of the total maximum points. Queen Bee means you found every single word.
